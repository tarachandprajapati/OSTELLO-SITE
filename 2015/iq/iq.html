-Erno Rubik

The Rubik’s cube, a puzzle that has continued and is continuing to fascinate people across all ages and professions since 40 years. People claim to have speeds under half a minute in cracking the cube, and it has over 43 quintillion possibilities that's 43,252,003,274,489,856,000.

So what about you?

ERNO KUBOZZ gives a platform for cubers to showcase their speed cubing talents, thus providing them a great chance of breaking several records. Following are the list of categories in which participants can showcase their talent.

ROUND 1:-
• 3x3 solving in 4 minutes will take you to next round.

ROUND 2:-
• 3x3 Speed solving and Selective Pattern Making.

BONUS ROUND:-
• 4x4 solving

1. Only Individual participation is allowed.
2. The decision of the judges and the organizing team will be final and binding.
3. If the number of participants qualified to round 2 are more, those who complete first will be qualified to the next round.
4. If there is a tie, the winner will be decided by bonus round.
5. Marking scheme will be announced right before the event starts.
