    Introduction

    A person may die, nations may rise and fall but an idea lives on forever. Such is the power of an idea. We at OSTELLO wish to acknowledge your idea by providing you with a platform to showcase our ideas and innovations in the form of projects and models. Any idea be it small or big is welcome. This event's goal is to give a tribune to all those scientific ideas hidden inside your brain waiting to be shared.     Details

    This event comprises of two rounds:-

    1. Abstract Submission:-

      2. Groups have to submit the Abstract in proper format based on which shortlisting will be done for second round.

    2. On Spot Presentation:-

      3. Groups have to present their models on spot.Further Notifications will be updated soon.

    Format of Abstract

    1. Team name and members.
    2. Objective of model or Idea of model.
    3. Brief description of model.
      • Block Diagram or Pictorial Representation.
      • Components used.
      • Working.
    4. Feasibility and future applications.

    Rules and Deadlines

    1. Team Size: Maximum of 5 members.
    2. Team members must register at Ojass'15 website.
    3. Last Date for Abstract Submission to exposicion@ojass.in: March 30, 2015.
    4. Result of first round(Abstract Submission Round): March 27, 2015.
